Across rural India, the lack of a single working toilet pushes thousands of
adolescent girls out of school every year. MayWe partners with government and
tribal schools to install and maintain the basics that make staying in school
possible: clean toilet blocks, handwash stations, and safe drinking water.

Every build is paired with awareness sessions on hygiene and menstrual health,
delivered with local teachers and women trained as **Hygiene Ambassadors**, so
the change outlasts the construction.
