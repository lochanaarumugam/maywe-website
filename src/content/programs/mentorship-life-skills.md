A scholarship opens the door; mentorship helps a girl walk through it. MayWe
connects beneficiaries with volunteer mentors — professionals and students who
give as little as two hours a month — for guidance on studies, careers and the
choices ahead.

Alongside one-to-one mentoring, we run workshops on life skills, leadership,
digital literacy and spoken English, helping girls build the confidence to
pursue higher education and leadership opportunities.
