Talent is everywhere; opportunity is not. MayWe scholarships back girls who have
already proven themselves academically but face the very real risk of dropping
out for financial reasons.

Selection is merit- and need-based, with clear eligibility criteria published
openly. Applications are reviewed each cycle, and sensitive applicant documents
are handled privately and deleted after the cycle closes.
